Overview

MacReach is a macOS application developed by Anton Stremovskiy (aSoft). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Short version: MacReach does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Everything stays on your device.

Third-party services

MacReach does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No SDKs that collect user data are embedded in the application.

If the app interacts with Apple services (such as iCloud or StoreKit for in-app purchases), those interactions are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Security

Since MacReach does not collect or transmit your data, there is no risk of your personal information being exposed through our servers. Any sensitive data (such as credentials, if applicable) is stored exclusively in the macOS Keychain using system-level encryption.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our end. You may uninstall MacReach at any time to remove all locally stored preferences from your device.

If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we collect no personal data, these rights are automatically satisfied. For any questions, please contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of MacReach after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
