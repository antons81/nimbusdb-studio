      <ul>

        <li>Connection is established via Bonjour (local network discovery)</li>

        <li>All communication happens directly between iPhone and Mac</li>

        <li>No relay servers, no cloud infrastructure</li>

        <li>Works offline — no internet connection required</li>

      </ul>

    </div>


    <div class="card">

      <h2><span class="icon">🔐</span> Security</h2>

      <p>MacReach uses a PIN-based authentication system to ensure only your devices can connect. The first time your iPhone connects to the Mac server, you must enter a 4-digit PIN shown on your Mac. Once verified, your device is remembered as trusted.</p>

      <ul>

        <li>PIN authentication for first-time connections</li>

        <li>Trusted device list stored locally on your Mac</li>

        <li>No passwords stored in plain text</li>

      </ul>

    </div>


    <div class="card">

      <h2><span class="icon">📋</span> What the App Accesses</h2>
