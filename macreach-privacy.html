Privacy Policy

MacReach is built with privacy at its core.
No data collection. No cloud. No accounts.

Last updated: May 26, 2026

🔒 No Data Collection

MacReach does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely on your local network and never communicates with any external servers.

No analytics No tracking No ads No accounts

📡 Local Network Only

MacReach communicates exclusively between your iPhone and your Mac on your local Wi-Fi network. All data — including volume levels, media controls, and battery information — stays on your devices and never leaves your home network.

  • Connection is established via Bonjour (local network discovery)
  • All communication happens directly between iPhone and Mac
  • No relay servers, no cloud infrastructure
  • Works offline — no internet connection required

🔐 Security

MacReach uses a PIN-based authentication system to ensure only your devices can connect. The first time your iPhone connects to the Mac server, you must enter a 4-digit PIN shown on your Mac. Once verified, your device is remembered as trusted.

  • PIN authentication for first-time connections
  • Trusted device list stored locally on your Mac
  • No passwords stored in plain text

📋 What the App Accesses

MacReach iOS app requests access to your local network solely to discover and communicate with the MacReach Server running on your Mac. No other data is accessed.

MacReach Server (macOS) accesses system audio controls and media playback to execute commands sent from your iPhone. All actions are performed locally on your Mac.

👶 Children's Privacy

MacReach does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data from any users, this applies equally to all age groups.
